This is a typeface that recreates the randomness of real calligraphy.

This typographic project is inspired by the natural and fluid forms of handwritten calligraphy, aiming to capture the essence of manual writing. Its main goal is to recreate the inherent randomness of the human writing process, a trait that brings authenticity and warmth.

The proposal seeks to break the boundaries between the digital and the tangible, emphasizing how imperfection and unpredictability connect both worlds. Through typography, it highlights randomness as an essential element that restores a human touch to design in an increasingly digitalized environment.
